## Project Details

### Project Title

**Studixa – AI-Powered Academic Operating System**

### Project Description

Studixa is an AI-powered academic platform designed to help students throughout their educational journey, from school to college and eventually their careers.

The platform combines AI mentorship, learning assistance, academic tracking, project management, internship discovery, skill development, career guidance, and personalized learning into a single ecosystem.

Students can access learning resources, generate notes and summaries using AI, track academic performance, manage projects and certificates, discover opportunities, and receive personalized guidance through an intelligent academic companion.

The goal of Studixa is to eliminate fragmentation in student learning and provide a centralized platform that supports complete academic and career growth.
